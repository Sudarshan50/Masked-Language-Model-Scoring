# Example sentences for plausibility scoring
# Lines starting with # are comments and will be ignored

# Highly plausible sentences
The cat sat on the mat.
She ate breakfast this morning.
He drove to work yesterday.

# Moderately plausible sentences
The artist painted a beautiful landscape.
They discussed the economic situation.

# Semantically odd but grammatical
The idea ate the computer.
The theory drove to happiness.
Colorless green ideas sleep furiously.

# Selectional preference violations
She drank the chair.
He painted the democracy.
The wall gave John the button.

# Physical impossibility
He put the mountain in his pocket.
She lifted the entire city.
They ate the building.
